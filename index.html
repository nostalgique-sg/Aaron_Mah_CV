About

An Industrial/Product designer whose practice centres on adhesive-free assembly — structures that hold through geometry, fold, and joinery alone. Proficient in conceptualising, creating visuals, 3D modelling, and renderings that communicate a design. Founder of a self-built design studio launched in under six months. Always learning the skills the field demands next.
